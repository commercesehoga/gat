GAT Overview — 5 Days

What is CUET GAT?
The General Aptitude Test (GAT) is Section III of CUET UG 2026. It tests logical reasoning, quantitative aptitude, GK, current affairs, and general mental ability. 50 questions · 60 minutes · +5 / –1 marking. All 50 questions are compulsory.

In triangle ABC, angle A = 50° and angle B = 60°. Find angle C.

If B = 4 and D = 8, E = 10, what is the code for J?
A12
B10
C11
D20 ✓
Pattern: Letter position × 2 (J=10th → 20)
Reasoning · Coding–Decoding
If BOY is coded DQA, how will CAT be coded?

Quant · Trigonometry
If tan A = 3/4, find the value of sin A.
3/5 (using Pythagoras: hyp = 5, sin = opp/hyp = 3/5)

Memory-Based Questions (Shift 1 & 2)
Quant · Profit & Loss
A sum of money is divided among A, B and C such that for every ₹2 A gets, B gets 65 paise and C gets 35 paise. If C's share is ₹560, find the total sum.
A₹2,800
B₹3,200 ✓
C₹4,000
D₹3,600
Ratio A:B:C = 200:65:35. C=35 parts=₹560 → 1 part=₹16 → Total=300×16=₹4,800... ₹3,200
Quant · Speed & Distance
Two trains 200 km apart start from opposite directions at the same time and cross each other at 110 km from one station. What is the ratio of their speeds?
11:9 (110 km vs 90 km covered in same time)
Quant · Least Perfect Square
Find the least perfect square divisible by 9, 12, 15, and 24.
A900
B1600
C3600 ✓
D6400
LCM(9,12,15,24)=360. Make perfect square: 360×10=3600=60²
Quant · Compound Interest
In how many years will ₹320 amount to ₹405 if compounded at 12.5% yearly?
A2 years ✓
B3 years
C2.5 years
D1 year
320×(1.125)² = 320×1.265625 = 405
Reasoning · Permutation
Using all the letters of the word 'SUNDAY', how many different words can be formed?
A6! = 720 ✓
B5! = 120
C7! = 5040
D8!
SUNDAY = 6 distinct letters → 6! = 720
Quant · Percentage
37.5% of 800 – 16⅔% of 1200 = ?
A80
B100 ✓
C120
D200
300 – 200 = 100
